Land Development Services We Specialize In

Land Grading

Land grading shapes and levels your property for proper drainage and stability. We expertly smooth and contour the land to prevent erosion and water pooling, ensuring a solid foundation for construction, landscaping, or other projects. Our experienced team uses state-of-the-art equipment to deliver precise results every time. Choosing us means protecting your investment and ensuring long-term success for your project.

Sod/Erosion Control

Lugo’s Precision Grading Services offers professional SOD installation and erosion control solutions for residential and commercial properties in Florida. Our expert team provides quality SOD installation to create lush, durable lawns, as well as effective erosion control techniques to protect your landscape from water and soil damage. Whether you need to stabilize a slope or prevent runoff, our erosion control services help maintain a healthy, sustainable property. Contact us today for reliable SOD and erosion control services in Florida.”

Land Clearing

Land clearing removes unwanted trees, shrubs, and debris from your property. Whether you're preparing for construction, farming, or just need to open up space, our land clearing services leave you with clean, usable land, tailored to your specific needs. With Lugo's Precision Grading, we prioritize efficiency and environmental responsibility, ensuring the job is done with minimal impact. Trust us to handle even the toughest jobs while delivering a clear, ready-to-use property.
