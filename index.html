Our Land Development Services

Site Preparation

Site preparation is the first step in any construction project. We clear, grade, and level the land to ensure it's ready for building. Our precise work creates a stable foundation, making your site safe and build-ready for any project.

Final Grading

Final Grading shapes and levels your property for proper drainage and stability. We expertly smooth and contour the land to prevent erosion and water pooling, ensuring a solid foundation for construction, landscaping, or other projects.

Land Clearing

Land clearing removes unwanted trees, shrubs, and debris from your property. Whether you’re preparing for construction, farming, or just need to open up space, our land clearing services leave you with clean, usable land, tailored to your specific needs.

Sod/Erosion Control

Lugo’s Precision Grading Services offers reliable erosion control solutions for residential and commercial properties in Florida. Our professional techniques prevent soil erosion and protect landscapes from runoff, ensuring lasting stability and sustainability.

Underground Drainage

Lugo’s Precision Grading Services delivers advanced, eco-conscious underground drainage solutions for residential and commercial properties throughout Florida. Using modern technology and sustainable practices, we efficiently manage stormwater, reduce flood risks, and safeguard your property’s foundation—while minimizing environmental impact.

Temp Drive Way/Access Road

Lugo’s Precision Grading Services delivers fast, compliant temporary driveway and access road installations across Florida. Designed to meet local regulations and project timelines, our access solutions ensure secure, reliable site entry with minimal delays.
