Site Preparation

Site preparation is the first step in any construction project. We clear, grade, and level the land to ensure it's ready for building. Our precise work creates a stable foundation, making your site safe and build-ready for any project.

Land Grading

Land grading shapes and levels your property for proper drainage and stability. We expertly smooth and contour the land to prevent erosion and water pooling, ensuring a solid foundation for construction, landscaping, or other projects.

Land Clearing

Land clearing removes unwanted trees, shrubs, and debris from your property. Whether you’re preparing for construction, farming, or just need to open up space, our land clearing services leave you with clean, usable land, tailored to your specific needs.

Sod/Erosion Control

Lugo’s Precision Grading Services offers reliable erosion control solutions for residential and commercial properties in Florida. Our professional techniques prevent soil erosion and protect landscapes from runoff, ensuring lasting stability and sustainability.
